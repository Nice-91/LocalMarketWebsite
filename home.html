The Problem

Farmers often don't know the current market price for crops like maize, beans, potatoes, and fruits. Buyers also risk paying too much or too little without accurate information.

The Solution

AgriPrice provides daily crop prices across Rwandan markets, helping farmers sell at fair prices and buyers make informed decisions.
